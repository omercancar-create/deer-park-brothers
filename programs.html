Weekly — Every Friday
Weekly
Halaqa

Our core weekly program. Brothers gather after Isha prayer for an Islamic circle — covering Quran tafsir, hadith, contemporary topics, and open Q&A. A space to learn, ask, and reflect together.

After Isha — approx. 9:00 PM
Deer Park Masjid
All brothers welcome

Monthly — Year Round
Community
Iftars

Sharing a meal is one of the greatest acts of brotherhood. Our community iftars bring brothers together to break fast, share stories, and strengthen the bonds that hold our community together.

Monthly — Saturday evenings
Community Hall, Deer Park
Brothers & families welcome

Weekly — For Ages 14–25
Youth
Programs

Dedicated to the next generation. Our youth programs combine Islamic education, mentorship, sports, and life-skills workshops — giving young Muslim men the tools, identity, and community they need to thrive.

Sundays — 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM
Deer Park Community Centre
