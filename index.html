Who We Are

A Place Where
Every Brother
Belongs

Deerpark Brothers is a Muslim community organisation in the heart of Deer Park, Melbourne. We exist to bring brothers together — creating spaces where faith is strengthened, friendships are formed, and every man finds a true sense of belonging.

From weekly Islamic circles to community iftars and youth programs, we build meaningful experiences that anchor brothers in their deen while connecting them to each other.
