Our Story

From a Small
Circle to a
Growing Family

Deerpark Brothers began with a handful of brothers gathering at a local masjid in Deer Park, Melbourne. United by faith and a shared love for their community, they wanted something more than just Friday prayers — they wanted a place where Muslim men could truly belong.

Over the years, that small circle grew into a thriving organisation. Today, Deerpark Brothers runs weekly halaqas, community iftars, youth programs, and more — all rooted in the same spirit that brought that first group together: faith, brotherhood, unity, and growth.

01
Faith

We are grounded in the Quran and Sunnah. Every program, event, and conversation is shaped by our love for Allah and His Messenger.

02
Brotherhood

Real brotherhood goes beyond surface-level friendship. We build bonds that support, challenge, and uplift one another through life's tests.

03
Unity

We welcome brothers of all backgrounds, ethnicities, and life stages. The masjid unites us, and our community keeps us together.

04
Growth

We are committed to continuous improvement — in our deen, our character, our relationships, and our contributions to the wider ummah.

Mission & Vision

Building the
Strongest Community

Our mission is to create a space where Muslim men in Deer Park and beyond can strengthen their faith, form meaningful bonds, and grow as individuals and as a community.

Our vision is a thriving Muslim community in Melbourne's west — one that is known for its warmth, its service, and its uncompromising commitment to Islamic values.
